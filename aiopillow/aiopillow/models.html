ESRGAN ("old Architecture") Models

Models that use the "old" ESRGAN architecture. They can be used either with the official ESRGAN repository (old arch tag) and BasicSR (old arch or preferably victorc's traiNNer version). You can also use tools that wrap around one of them, like IEU from Honh or Cupscale by Nmkd. A new tool that allows chaining various tasks (such as image scaling, brightness/contrast editing, and comparison generation) with ESRGAN models is chaiNNer by Joey.
